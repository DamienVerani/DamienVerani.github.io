Family Memory

About The Game

The goal of this game is to find the pairs by memorizing the position of the cards. The cards are pictures of the patient's family, due to the personalised cards, the game is more efficient.

How To Play ?

At the beginning, all the cards are face down, firstly, the patient has to pick up one card to reveal. After that, he has to find the second member of the pairs(the same card) between the cards left. If the patient pick up the right one the pair disappears else the two cards picked up come back to the face down position. Then repeat this actons until all the cards disappear.

Improvment

We have created different level to adapt this game to the patient needs and patient abilities. The difficuly of the game increased with the number of pairs the patient has to find. Finally, the hardest mode consist of the use of pairs that aren't the same, the difficulty here is to find pairs of same people even if the pictures are different.

Pathfinder

About The Game

Pathfinder is a game design to train your attention and problem-solving abilities. The goal is to lead the balls in the pipes to the box of the same color by changing the direction of the intersection.

How To Play ?

The game starts with a 3-minute cooldown, multiples boxes are displayed on the screen with a pipe that splits ways to lead to them. The balls come through the pipe with a color and takes the path created by the pipes, once they arrive at an intersection, they follow the direction of intersection (displayed with an arrow and a wall blocking one of the paths). To change direction of an intersection, you can click on it. At the end of the timer, your score is displayed showing how many balls you manage to lead to the right box.

Improvment

The game difficulty can be increased or decreased by changing the speed of the ball, the number of colors and the complexity of the paths. It is set automatically based on the performances of the patient but can be change at the before the game.
